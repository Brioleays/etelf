Who should attend this seminar?

  • People who are concerned about long-term care costs and future healthcare needs
  • Individuals or families who currently have any estate planning documents in place, or the documents you have are more than three years old
  • Individuals wanting to learn how to maintain control and access to your assets, even in unexpected situations.
